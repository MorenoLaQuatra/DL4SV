## Course Overview

<div class="objectives">

### Learning Objectives
- Understand fundamental concepts of deep learning
- Master convolutional neural networks for vision tasks
- Learn transformer architectures for sequential data
- Apply deep learning to speech and vision problems
- Gain practical experience with PyTorch and modern tools

</div>

---

## Course Structure

<div class="header">

### Modules

1. **Introduction to Deep Learning** - Fundamentals and optimization
2. **Convolutional Neural Networks** - Computer vision architectures
3. **Transformers** - Attention mechanisms and modern architectures
4. **Deep Learning Libraries** - PyTorch, HuggingFace, monitoring tools
5. **Applications** - Practical projects in speech and vision

</div>

## Why Deep Learning?

<div class="grid-2">

<div class="definition">

### Traditional Approaches

- Manual feature engineering
- Domain expertise required
- Limited capacity
- Difficult to adapt

</div>

<div class="example">

### Deep Learning

- Automatic feature learning
- Data-driven
- Scales with data
- Generalizes across domains

</div>
